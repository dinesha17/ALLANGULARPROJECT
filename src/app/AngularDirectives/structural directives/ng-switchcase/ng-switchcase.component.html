The ngSwitch is an Angular structural directive, which allows us to add or remove DOM elements. It works in conjunction with ngSwitchcase, & ngSwitchDefault directives. It is similar to the switch statement of JavaScript. In this tutorial, we will look at the syntax of ngSwitch, ngSwitchcase & ngSwitchDefault. We will show you how to use these directives using an example. The examples include ngSwitch Example, multiple ngSwitchCase , Loose Equality Checks, etc.
